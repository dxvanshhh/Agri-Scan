About Agri-Scan

This project is an AI-powered diagnostic tool designed to help farmers instantly identify crop diseases and get actionable insights.

Core Features:

  • Instant AI Diagnosis: Our core engine is trained on the Kaggle PlantVillage dataset to identify multiple diseases across several crops.
  • Actionable Insights: We don't just give a label; we provide immediate, expert-level recommendations for treatment and prevention.
  • "Flaw-Proof" Safety: Our model includes a 90% confidence threshold to prevent "wrong answers."

Our Multi-Layered AI Engine

  • Model A (Leaf Scan): A MobileNetV2 classification model to identify specific diseases from a single leaf.
  • Model B (Field Scan): A U-Net segmentation model to identify patterns of nutrient stress across a wide field.
